VFX Projects

Here you can see all of my finished VFX's I have created

VFX Module video

The video above shows the VFX's' I created during my 9 week module of learning VFX in Unreal Engine.

I really enjoyed learning how to create VFX in Unreal Engine and I definitely plan on continuning my learning in the near future.

What are the VFX's I created?

I created a character to assist me with the creation of my VFX's. The character I thought of was a goblin king who came from a distant world.

Effect 1: The first effect I created was a 'Special Ability Effect'. This effect spawns an orb around the player which is supposed to act as a defensive shield. An effect then spawns from the ground which resembles a portal to the goblins home planet. Once this effect was created, I couldn't help but feel as though something was missing so I added in small orbs that spawn above the portal.

Effect 2: My second effect was an enviromental effect. I decided to create an eye that blinks. In a working game, I would have liked to have the eyes follow the player as they move around the map. This effect is relevant to my character as I had the idea that behind the eyes are the goblin kings companions ensuring he is safe on his mission. To create this effect I followed a YouTube tutorial mainly for the blink mechanism. Along with this, I also created a swirl effect that I placed around the eye with a beam that appears to exit the pupil.

Effect 3: The third effect I created is a healing effect. To create this I made a flipbook of crosses and made them spawn a random section of the flipbook in a random area between the boundry. The boundry is my own design too of which i created using a kaleidoscope website. I created this to be black and white and had to use a website to remove the background so I was able to change the colour of it in Unreal. The boundry slowly rotates. The final part of my heal effect is the beams that slowly rise from the ground.

Effect 4: My fourth effect is a jump effect. This one was really simple make once I managed to get the sparks to act how I want them to, however I chose to do this because I wanted to test myself with the timing in blueprint. As you can see in the video, the timing was still a little bit off unfortunately, but I do wish to revisit it and fix it.

Effect 5: My final effect is my recreation of the Valorant spike plant. I thoroughly enjoyed creating this effect because I loved playing around with the spawn time and making the whole effect work in correct time.
